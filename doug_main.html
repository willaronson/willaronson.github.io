The Trouble With Doug

lyrics by Daniel Maté
music by Will Aronson
book by Will Aronson and Daniel Maté

The Trouble With Doug is a contemporary comedic re-imagining of Franz Kafka’s short story “The Metamorphosis.” On the eve of his 27th birthday, Doug Gregory transforms from a happy and healthy young man into a giant garden slug. Family relationships undergo their own surprising metamorphoses as the Gregory clan confronts the loss of the son they knew.

The show was presented at the 2010 NAMT Festival of New Musicals under the direction of Victoria Clark, and has also been developed at CAP21, Royal & Derngate, and TheatreWorks (Palo Alto). In April 2014, a production of Doug opened at the Theater at Arts Garage in Delray Beach, FL.
