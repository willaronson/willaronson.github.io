Will Aronson recently composed the score and co-wrote the book for the Korean musical Bungee Jump (multiple Seoul productions), cited last year by the NY Times as Korea’s “most popular original musical,” and winner of Best Score at the 2012 Korea Musical Awards. He has also written the scores for Mother, Me and the Monsters (Barrington Stage; Boston Globe Critic's Pick), My Scary Girl (Best Musical, 2009 New York Musical Theatre Festival), and The Trouble With Doug (NAMT 2010; Theatreworks Palo Alto), for which he also co-wrote the book. Current projects include Hansel & Gretl & Heidi & Günter (2014 NAMT development grant), commissioned by Chicago Shakespeare Theater, and an adaptation of the children's book series Pete the Cat for Theatreworks USA. Other work includes music for Mary Testa’s Sleepless Variations and the vocal and dance arrangements for the William Finn/James Lapine musical, Little Miss Sunshine (Second Stage Theatre). Will is the recipient of a Fulbright grant, an ASCAP Frederick Loewe Award, and a Baryshnikov Fellowship, and is a 2015-2016 resident artist at Ars Nova Uncharted (with librettist Sarah Hammond). He holds a B.A. from Harvard University and an M.F.A. from Tisch/NYU. 

In addition to his theatrical work, Will has composed and produced nearly 100 tracks for the best-selling Korean children's book/DVD series, English Egg. www.englishegg.co.kr
