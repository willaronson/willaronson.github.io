"This disarming mix of chamber-pop, earnest romantic comedy and creepy horror may have been the festival's most original find... Will Aronson's score, set to Kang's lyrics, is sunny and tuneful.  The ballads manage sincerity without treacle, and the uptempo numbers bounce without breaking.  With a stronger - and longer - libretto, this 'hor-romantic' comedy could be a real crossover sensation.  Either way, we're glad we caught it." 

- Rob Weinert-Kendt, American Theatre, January 2010 

"I had a wonderful time... the music was terrific... 90 minutes, no intermission, and I was with it all the way... so far, for me, it's the leader in the festival."
